# Privacy Policy

**Respite (小憩)**

Last updated: March 11, 2026

## Overview

Respite ("the App") is a desktop break reminder application developed by Xiaohua Pan ("we", "us", "our"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data Collection

**Respite does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.** The App runs entirely on your local device.

Specifically, the App does **not**:

- Collect any personally identifiable information
- Track your usage behavior or analytics
- Access your contacts, photos, location, or any other personal data
- Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
- Send any data to external servers
- Include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs

## Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **User preferences** — such as your selected scenario, timer settings, and display options
- **Scenario configurations** — built-in and custom break scenarios you create

This data is stored in the application's local data directory and is never transmitted off your device. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App.

## Network Access

The App does not make any network requests. The network entitlement is included solely for potential future functionality (e.g., checking for updates) and is not currently used.

## Children's Privacy

Since the App does not collect any data, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. The App is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
